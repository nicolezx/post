Description-long:

*Sent*, the publication, celebrates 100 ideas about space. The project (2019-2021) brought more voices and experiences into the conversation about space. The publication is a holistic review and includes all postcards accompanied by an introduction to the contributors, additional letters and material from the AA Summer School Unit, *With Love*. Presented as a box of treasures *Sent* has the playful intention to inspire and delight.
